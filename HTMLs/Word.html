About Word

Microsoft Word: It is one of the programs provided within the Office package and is dedicated to word processing and so that it allows the words to be entered in electronic format on virtual documents and then processed and output as required according to the user's need so that they can be printed on paper sheets or kept in their electronic form, and it is one of the most important programs that have been produced by Microsoft, the American word processing company. The first version of it was in 1983 under the name Multi-Tool Word, and this program was dedicated to the Zenex operating system, which is one of the versions of Unix licensed by AT&T phone laboratories, which was sold and promoted by Microsoft before the release of its other famous systems for personal computers.

Key Features of MS Word

  • Working With Text and Styles
  • Saving and Printing
  • Word and Compatibility
  • Built-in Collaboration
