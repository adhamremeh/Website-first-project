How To Download PowerPoint

you must do the following

  1. Download PowerPoint Mobile from theMicrosoft Store,for free
  2. Start the Microsoft Store by clicking or tapping on its shortcut from the Start Menu.
  3. Then search for PowerPoint: when the results are shown, click or tap on PowerPoint Mobile. If you don’t see it in your results, you can use this direct link to PowerPoint Mobile. On the app’s page, click or tap on Ins.
  4. Wait for PowerPoint Mobile to download and install. It shouldn’t take long, as it doesn’t have a large size: only about 50MB.
  5. When the installation is over, click or tap on Launch to open PowerPoint Mobile. Later on, you can start it using its shortcut from the Start Menu.
